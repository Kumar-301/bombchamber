BRIDGE BETWEEN INDIA & THE WORLD

Establishment of Bombay Chamber of Commerce and Industry

The Chamber was founded in 1836 by seven Britishers and three Parsees. The Chamber has played a significant role in supporting the development of industries in Mumbai over the last several decades. The Chamber serves as an effective vehicle of communication between the regulatory bodies, the corporate and the society. It collaborates with other Industry associations and thus increases its reach to the decision makers beyond boundaries of Mumbai.
